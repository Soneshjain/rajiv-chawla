Everything you need to know about GAPS Basic

GAPS Basic is an exclusive digital community for SME business owners curated by Rajiv K Chawla. It's designed to provide direct access to expert guidance, curated information, a supportive network, and cost-saving benefits to help your business grow.

GAPS Basic offers a unique advantage: direct engagement with Rajiv K Chawla, a seasoned entrepreneur with over four decades of experience building a successful manufacturing business. You'll receive his personal insights and have your specific queries addressed. Additionally, the curated information, exclusive benefits like the diesel discount and international training nominations, and the focus on tangible support set it apart.

While Rajiv personally oversees the community and provides high-quality answers to your posted queries, the frequency of his direct individual responses will depend on the volume of questions. However, he actively participates in the community and ensures valuable insights are shared regularly.

You'll gain access to carefully curated information on regular industry updates, crucial policy changes, relevant government circulars and schemes, and financial opportunities like incentives, subsidies, loans, and Cash Credit limits.

We aim to host in-person Gala Dinners at least once every quarter. These events provide invaluable opportunities to network with fellow SME owners, connect with Rajiv K Chawla in person, celebrate successes, and foster a strong sense of community.

Each month, we'll host online seminars covering a wide range of topics crucial for SME growth, such as finance, marketing, operations, and leadership. These sessions will feature expert speakers and provide actionable insights you can implement in your business.

Upon becoming a GAPS Basic member, you'll receive details on how to access the attractive discounts we've negotiated with our partner insurance providers. For the diesel discount (₹1/- per litre with free door delivery), we will provide you with the necessary contact information and procedures to avail this benefit in your operational areas.

While we cannot guarantee the recovery of all stuck payments, Rajiv K Chawla and the community will provide guidance, share strategies, and offer insights based on experience to help you navigate this challenging issue more effectively.

From time to time, GAPS Basic members will receive exclusive invitations to nominate themselves or their team members for fully sponsored international training programs. These programs are designed to provide exposure to global best practices and enhance your business acumen.

You can seek guidance on a wide array of business aspects, including credit facilitation, financial literacy, fundraising, import-export procedures, intellectual property rights (patents, copyrights, trademarks), succession planning, and overall business growth strategies.

We will periodically identify common needs within the GAPS Basic community for raw materials, machinery, consumables, and office equipment. By aggregating demand, we aim to negotiate bulk discounts with suppliers, passing on significant cost savings to our members.

GAPS Basic will provide a platform and guidance on how to effectively channel your suggestions and grievances related to policy, GST, labour laws, PF, and other government regulations to the relevant authorities.
